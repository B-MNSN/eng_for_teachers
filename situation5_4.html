สถานการณ์ที่ 5 ห้องพักครู

ถาม – ตอบการคัดเลือกนักเรียนตัวแทน

T1 : Have you already chosen the student to receive Poh Teck Tung scholarships (a)?

T2 : I haven’t chosen yet. What are its criterion?

T1 : The notice mentioned that the students must have the good behavior (b).

T2 : Will it expire tomorrow (c)? Which documents do we have to prepare?

T1 : Yes. You have to prepare the scholarship applications (d).

เลือกสำหรับส่วน a:

A Merit scholarships
B BMA Education Fund Scholarship
C Wat Phra Si Mahatat Scholarship
D TISCO Foundation Scholarship
E Waterdrop Foundation Scholarship
F Scholarship under the project of students notifying more than one hundred bad effects for more than one million scholarships
G Grant for fire victims
H IPEC Scholarship
I Zero Dropout Scholarship
J Scholarship under the project of youths touching the life under Thai seas for scuba diving learning

เลือกสำหรับส่วน b:

A the students must have GPA more than 3.00
B the students are poor.
C the students have special skills.
D the students have to encounter the house fire.
E the students must write the bad effects of marijuana and drug relevantly in complete sentences and can express the meanings clearly.
F the students must participate in many campaigns supporting youths to stay away from drugs.
G the students’ families must be very poor. Their monthly average income are about 1,094 baht per person.
H the students must write essays about the sea.

เลือกสำหรับส่วน c:

A next week
B next Monday
C next month

เลือกสำหรับส่วน d:

A Copy of ID card
B Affirmation of School
C Copy of school-record in the latest semester
D Recommendation letter from advisor
E Straight face photo
F Photo of lodgings
G Letter in one page of A4 to truly describe about living and lodging conditions
H English test results
I Income certificate of parents
