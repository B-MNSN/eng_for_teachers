สถานการณ์ที่ 5 ห้องพักครู

การพูดคุยกับเพื่อนครูเกี่ยวกับกิจกรรมในโรงเรียน

T1 : What activities will there be in our school next week? (a)

T2 : Students will have O-NET Test (b).

T1 : What can I do for you?

T2 : In the morning. Can you help me to tutor them? (c)

T1 : Certainly.

T2 : Thank you.

เลือกสำหรับส่วน a:

A Next month
B Tomorrow

เลือกสำหรับส่วน b:

A Students have RT Test.
B Students have NT Test.
C Students have Pre O-NET Test.
D Students have GAT Test.
E Students have PAT Test.
F Students have the final exam.
G Students have the practical examination.
H Students have the speaking test.
I Students have the listening test.
J Students have the writing test.
K Students have the reading test.
L Students have the midterm test.
M Students have the posttest.
N Students are tested after learning vocabularies by heart.
O Students have the conversation test in many situations.
P Students have the dhamma study exam.

เลือกสำหรับส่วน c:

A manage exams.
B set the examination questions.
C photocopy test papers.
E arrange examination rooms.
F hand out test papers to each examination room.
G put test papers in envelops.
H prepare appliances used in the examination like staplers, staples, and face sheets
I collect test papers after the examination finished.
J fill the marks in.
K verify test papers.
